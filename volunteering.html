The Good Scientists

The Good Scientists is a collaborative initiative by young professionals aiming to connect non-profits with scientific experts for pro bono advice, thereby enhancing direct interactions to create societal impact. We highlight the potential of student involvement in making science and technology accessible and are working to compile resources on responsible licensing. Our platforms are designed to foster dialogue between civil society and scientists, with an overarching goal to establish a community sharing best practices in this cross-sector collaboration.

Datapizza

Datapizza is the largest Italian Tech, Data Science, and AI community with a mission to educate about Data and AI and guide people towards 21st-century careers. Recognizing that many young people are unaware of the opportunities AI brings, they offer seminars for high school students to introduct them to this new disruptive technology.
